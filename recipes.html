Spaghetti Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish known for its simplicity and rich flavor. Cooked al dente spaghetti is tossed with a creamy sauce made from eggs, grated Pecorino Romano cheese, black pepper, and crispy pancetta or guanciale. The result is a satisfying and indulgent pasta dish with a perfect balance of creaminess and savory goodness.

Cobb Salad

The Cobb salad is a classic American dish featuring a bed of crisp salad greens topped with neatly arranged rows of grilled chicken, hard-boiled eggs, bacon, avocado slices, diced tomatoes, and crumbled blue cheese. The salad is finished with a drizzle of vinaigrette or blue cheese dressing, creating a delicious and visually appealing meal.
